Jim Henson, the Man behind the Muppet

man or muppet?

Jim Henson was born on September 24, 1936 in Greenville, Mississippi, full name James Maury Henson, was an American puppeteer, animator, cartoonist, actor, inventor, and filmmaker who achieved worldwide notability as the creator of the Muppets. Henson was also well known for creating Fraggle Rock and as the director of The Dark Crystal and Labyrinth
