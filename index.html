Jim Henson, the Man behind the Muppet

man or muppet?

Jim Henson was born on September 24, 1936 in Greenville, Mississippi, and passed away on May 16, 1990 full name James Maury Henson, was an American puppeteer, animator, cartoonist, actor, inventor, and filmmaker who went on to create the Muppets. The Muppets were a group of diverse characters including Kermit the Frog, Miss Piggy, Fozzie Bear, and Gonzo. His puppetry took off as an early teenager, starring on local television shows.
